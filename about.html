This is why we do it:


All over our native country of Kenya, there are men, women and children without a roof over their heads, no access to food and clean water and without any real prospects of bettering their futures. Most of them are "..just waiting to die..."

We find ourselves as Co-Charity in a position to help these persons out and start them off on a new life with their dignity intact.

With the money we collect, we cloth and feed The Homeless of Kenya. We then put them up in various collaborating centers across the country where these unfortunates finally have a place to sleep safe from nature's elements.

We give them all sorts of hands-on training that enables them to make some income and once tested and proven, we organise jobs and alternative housing for them.

Of course this is never the end of the friendships we form, as we remain closely working with our former sufferers; through good and bad, scarcity or plenty.



Help us and help them!
